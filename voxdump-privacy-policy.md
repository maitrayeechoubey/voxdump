# Voxdump Privacy Policy

Voxdump does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All voice input is transcribed on-device using Apple's Speech framework. All task parsing is performed on-device using Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models framework). No audio, transcripts, or task data ever leaves your device.

The app has no backend server, no analytics, no advertising, and no account system.
